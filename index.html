About Lamarck Biosciences

We created Lamarck Biosciences because we wanted to make the cutting edge research, that has been available so far for medical or scientific uses only, accessible for industrial applications. There is a huge gap between academic and industrial research in the area of bioinformatics and computational biology and we want to fill in this gap by offering clients from the food and beverage industry tailored, end-to-end solutions. Data obtained from such analyses will give producers insights that have not been attainable before, therefore providing them with necessary tools to make their technological and R&D processes as innovative as they can possibly be putting them at the forefront of new industrial revolution.

We have a thorough knowledge about the Mediterranean food scene, which has allowed us to carve strong relationships with some key players.

We help high-end producers to understand and exploit natural variability arising in elements such as soils, plant microbiomes, fermentation or dry curing methods to develop and improve artisan goods.

We collaborate with industrial clients to reduce defects in food production chains by implementing novel high-throughput metrics that allow for disciplined six-sigma quality management.

We work with large demanding retailers that are passionate about good stuff to anticipate global and seasonal market trends by using machine learning and large-scale natural language processing.

Precision viticulture and enology

Metagenomics

Metagenomics is a powerful, high-throughput tool that makes it possible to analyse a range of diverse microsystems as deep as at a strain level.

DNA sequencing is the future of medicine, and we strongly believe that it will also revolutionise food and beverage industry. State of the art equipment and complex analytical methods allow us to decipher environments such as air, soil, liquids, plants or food with a precision so far unattainable with standard laboratory techniques.

Data obtained from such analysis in turn can be used to optimise and improve production processes as well as eliminate unnecessary waste due to spoilage organisms or pathogens involvement.

Quality and safety control have never been so cutting edge. Our tailored end-to-end solutions will allow producers to stay ahead of their competition.

Customized probiotic dairy

Metabolomics

Metabolomics allows to identify, quantify and analyse various metabolites in different types of biological samples.

Quality, safety and authenticity of foods and beverages can be investigated with great precision with the detailed results molecular fingerprinting provides.

Using data from metabolomics analysis producers can improve organoleptic properties of their products such as aromas and flavours and increase their nutritional value with high-sensitivity methods and customised pipelines.

What is more a detection of minute changes in the composition of products ingredients can be traced using metabolomics analysis providing a precise tool for assessing their authenticity.
